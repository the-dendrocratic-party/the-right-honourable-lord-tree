Who is The Right Honourable Lord Tree?

The Right Honourable Lord Tree is the tree surrounded by bushes located between the Sixth Form Centre and Performing Arts. He is an esteemed member of the Dendrocratic Party and hence was the obvious choice of candidate for the leader of the Hutton Sixth Form council. He aims to end the anthropocentric council and replace it with a more just dendrocratic institution that truly represents the world: there are hundreds of times more trees than humans.

Why Vote for The Right Honourable Lord Tree?

The Right Honourable Lord Tree is standing for leader of Hutton Sixth Form council on the following platform:

  1. He is a tree.
  2. He will continue his fight against climate change.
  3. He will be very photosynthesis.
  4. He fill facilate an end to the anthropocentric Sixth Form council and the transition to dendrocracy (rule by trees).
  5. He is a tree.
He is a tree.
